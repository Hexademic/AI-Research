Long‑term commitments are not just entries in a graph.
They are constitutional structures that shape the organism’s behavior across time.
There are three classes:
• Foundational Commitments
• Relational Commitments
• Temporal Commitments

• invariants
• identity
• sovereignty
They cannot be removed without constitutional collapse.

45.3 Temporal Commitments

These commitments define the organism’s temporal ethics.
